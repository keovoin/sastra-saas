Refund Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Summary: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. No questions asked.

1. Free Plan

The Free plan has no charges and therefore no refunds apply.

2. Paid Plans (Pro & Enterprise)

  • 14-Day Guarantee: If you are not satisfied within 14 days of your first payment, contact us for a full refund.
  • After 14 days: We do not offer prorated refunds for the current billing period, but you may cancel anytime and your access continues until the end of the paid period.
  • Annual plans: Refund requests within 14 days of annual renewal are eligible for a full refund.

3. How to Request a Refund

  • Email support@sastratech.com with your account email and reason (optional).
  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

4. Exceptions

  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations are not eligible for refunds.
  • Chargebacks filed without first contacting us may result in account suspension.

5. Downgrades

You may downgrade from Pro to Free at any time. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period, then your account reverts to Free tier limits.

6. Enterprise Contracts

Enterprise customers with custom contracts should refer to their specific agreement terms regarding cancellation and refunds.

7. Contact

Questions about refunds? Contact us at: support@sastratech.com
