Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps, and device information via Vercel Analytics.
  • Content Data: Projects, tasks, risks, invoices, and other business data you create within the platform.
  • AI Configuration: Your AI provider selection and model preferences (stored locally in your browser only — NOT on our servers).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To authenticate you and protect your account.
  • To send transactional emails (invites, password resets).
  • To analyze usage patterns for product improvement.
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS) with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Row Level Security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own project data.
  • AI API keys are stored ONLY in your browser's localStorage — never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • We use HTTPS/TLS for all communications.

4. AI Features & Third-Party Services

  • AI features use YOUR configured API key to communicate with providers like Groq, OpenRouter, or Google AI Studio.
  • Data sent to AI providers is subject to THEIR privacy policies.
  • We route AI requests through a server proxy for CORS compatibility — we do not log or store the content of AI requests/responses.
  • Analytics: Vercel Analytics (anonymized page views, Web Vitals).
  • Authentication: Supabase Auth (email/password, OAuth).

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with third parties except:

  • With your consent (e.g., when you share a project with team members).
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid legal processes.
  • With service providers essential to operation (Supabase, Vercel) under strict data processing agreements.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the platform.
  • Export: You can export your data at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Correction: You can update your profile information at any time.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. After account deletion, data is permanently removed within 30 days. Backups are purged within 90 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Vercel Analytics uses privacy-friendly, cookieless tracking.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in regions where our service providers operate (US, EU). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Material changes will be communicated via email.

12. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@sastratech.com
